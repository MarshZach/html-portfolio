The Best Movies According to Zach

Top 3 movies of all-time.

Samurai Trilogy

Love the complete story of a ronan's growth into a samurai. Lots of great morals throughout

Dr Strangelove or: How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love the Bomb

Really great satirical take on the horrors of the atomic bomb during the Cold War.

Charade

Witty 'who dun it film' with a nice twist
